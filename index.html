Our Story

Friendship, Relationship

2014

Our relationship is truly one for the 21st Century and beyond.

We met through an online dating app in 2014 called OKCupid. We had our first date on April 3, 2014. Exactly two years later, after learning we were meant for each other through the ups and downs of a relationship, we were engaged.

We had a Hindu ceremony for our engagement in September 2016, and held a beautiful wedding in the auspicious city of Puri, in the state of Odisha in India, with more than 300 of our family and friends.


--Vikaas Shanker and Lora Khuntia
