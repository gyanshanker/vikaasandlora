Our Engagement

The Proposal

In March of 2016, after we both moved from the Chicago area to California, Vikaas surprised Lora with a trip to Disneyland, which we took a week later. Going to Disneyland was one of Lora’s dreams. We both spent three full days enjoying meeting our favorite childhood heroes and heroines.

But in the surprise was another surprise.

On April 3, 2016, exactly two years after our first date, Vikaas kneeled in front of his princess, in front of Sleeping Beauty Castle, and proposed. Lora said “yes!

The Party

On Sept. 3 and 4, Lora’s parents hosted a religious Hindu engagement ceremony and party for us in Mokena, Illinois.

Dozens of our friends and family came to give us their wishes. During the religious ceremony, or puja, on Sept. 3, close family traveled from different states to give us their blessings. The next night, we had a large party that featured home-cooked Indian food, a cultural program, and a meeting of both extended families.
